About Us

Our Risk Management Unit is committed to fostering a proactive risk-aware culture across the University and its Strategic Business Units (SBUs). Our mandate is to provide effective risk oversight and guidance, ensuring that potential risks are identified, assessed, and addressed in a timely and efficient manner. We believe that risk management is everyone's responsibility. As such, we are dedicated to equipping staff members across the University and its SBUs with the necessary knowledge and skills to become risk-conscious and actively contribute to the prevention and mitigation of risks in their respective roles.

Our team continuously monitors and evaluates the effectiveness of risk management strategies to ensure they remain relevant and responsive to the dynamic operating environment of the University. We are also focused on establishing robust systems for early identification and reporting of potential business risks, allowing management to take informed and timely remedial actions. Through collaboration, training, and continuous improvement, we strive to create a safe, resilient, and sustainable environment where CUT can thrive in the face of uncertainty
